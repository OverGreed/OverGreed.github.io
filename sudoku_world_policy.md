# Sudoku World Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-17

## Overview

Sudoku World is a fantasy-themed Sudoku game for Android, with Web builds used mainly for testing and development workflows. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the app, how that information is used, and what choices you have.

The app does not require you to create an account and does not operate its own gameplay backend for player profiles or cloud saves. Most gameplay data is stored locally on your device. Some information may be processed by third-party platform and monetization services, especially Google AdMob and Google Play.

## Information We Process

### 1. Information stored locally on your device

Sudoku World stores gameplay and preference data locally to make the app work as expected. Depending on the features you use, this may include:

- active game session data so you can resume a puzzle
- adventure progression and level completion state
- available hints and rewarded hint state
- app settings, including notification preferences and tutorial state
- monetization state stored on device, such as post-game ad cooldown timestamps
- locally cached purchase entitlement state, including pending or restored Remove Ads status
- reminder activity timestamps used to decide whether a local return reminder should be shown

This local data stays on your device unless you remove it by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

### 2. Advertising data processed by Google AdMob

Sudoku World uses Google AdMob to load and show ads. In the current product design, this includes post-game ads and optional rewarded ads that can grant an extra hint.

When AdMob is used, Google and its partners may collect or receive information such as:

- device identifiers and advertising identifiers
- IP address and approximate location derived from it
- device, app, and network information
- ad request and ad interaction data
- diagnostic, fraud-prevention, and performance information
- information used by Google to deliver, limit, measure, and diagnose ads; Google may determine whether ads are personalized or non-personalized under its own systems, applicable law, and your Google or device settings

AdMob data processing is governed by Google's own privacy terms and policies.

## 3. Purchase data processed by Google Play Billing

On supported Android release builds, Sudoku World uses Google Play Billing for the Remove Ads purchase and purchase restore flow.

When you make or restore a purchase, Google Play may process information such as:

- product and transaction metadata
- purchase tokens or order identifiers
- entitlement and ownership status
- country, region, or tax-related information required by Google Play
- fraud-prevention, security, and compliance information

Sudoku World stores only the local entitlement state needed to reflect whether Remove Ads is active or pending on your device. Payment processing is handled by Google Play, not by the app itself.

## 4. Notification and reminder data

If you enable notifications, Sudoku World may schedule local reminders on your device to encourage you to return to the game after inactivity.

For this feature, the app may process:

- whether notifications are enabled
- your last played timestamp
- the last reminder timestamp
- local notification scheduling metadata on your device

Sudoku World does not require its own remote push-notification backend for this feature.
On Android, scheduled local reminders may remain scheduled across device restart or app update until they are shown, replaced, cancelled, disabled, or removed with the app's data.

## 5. Information we do not intentionally collect directly

Sudoku World does not currently require direct account registration, username creation, or an in-app profile. The app also does not include a dedicated first-party analytics backend in this repository.

That said, third-party services such as Google AdMob and Google Play may process information under their own policies when you use monetization features.

## How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- run the game and restore local progress
- keep your settings and gameplay state available between sessions
- manage optional ads and enforce ad cooldown rules
- grant rewarded hints after successful rewarded ad views
- activate, restore, and remember the Remove Ads entitlement
- schedule local return reminders when you have enabled notifications
- maintain app security, fraud prevention, and service reliability through platform and third-party SDK features

## How Information Is Shared

Information may be shared or made available to third parties only as needed to operate the app features you use, including:

- Google AdMob for advertising delivery and measurement
- Google Play for billing, purchase validation, and entitlement management
- Android platform notification services for local notification delivery

If required by law, information may also be disclosed to comply with legal obligations, enforce rights, or protect users and the app.

## Data Retention

- Local gameplay and settings data remains on your device until you clear app storage, reinstall the app, or the app overwrites older state with newer state.
- Purchase records processed by Google Play are retained according to Google Play's own policies.
- Ad-related data processed by Google is retained according to Google's own policies.
- Locally scheduled reminders remain on your device until they are shown, replaced, cancelled, or removed with the app's data.

## Your Choices and Controls

You can control or limit data processing in several ways:

- disable notifications in the app's settings or through Android system settings
- purchase Remove Ads to disable eligible post-game ads in the app; optional rewarded ads may still remain available as a separate feature
- reset locally stored gameplay data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app
- manage Google's advertising and privacy controls through your Google account, Android settings, and Google's own privacy tools where available
- manage Google Play purchase history and payment settings through your Google Play account
